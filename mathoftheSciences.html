Reccommended Texts or Study Materials

Mathematical physics/Spectral Theory:

Everything by Barry Simon, Jake Fillman, Millivoje Lukic, Rui Han, Jean Bourgain, Svetlana Jitomirskaya, Schlag, Avila.

Foundational Tools/Techniques/Ideas:

Broadly and loosely speaking, the area cares about understanding:
1. Existence of solutions, and whether or not we can obtain significant spectral information from it.
2. Given existence of solutions, what information its propagation speed can tell us about its spectral properties.
3. From the dynamical point-of-view, how to map the transition phases of certain well-known physical models and their generations; see, Anderson localization.
4. Due to the vast amount of work carried on this area, many tools from number theory, ergodic theory, etc. are often necessary, so people like to get philosophical about the implications.
